ABOUT ME

Information professional with an integrated background of technology, design, and humanities.

VIEW MY RESUME

Hi, I am Siyu, currently a master student at the University of Michigan's School of Information, specializing in Human-computer Interaction, which is a fusion of my intellectual interests: technology, design, and people.

My professional experience has spanned media, digital marketing, and technology start-ups. I am a former journalist. Working in the media sector for four years helped me develop storytelling abilities and a keen eye for judging and extracting core information. I gained hands-on experience in content management, website design, and application development when serving as the project manager to build up a new media product. Prior to starting my master's program, I worked for a Hong Kong-based social media marketing company.

I am a strong believer in interdisciplinary learning. Consolidating my expertise in multiple disciplines, I aim to apply design thinking to solve problems and to apply data science to uncover insights.

Thank you for visiting my website, feel free to get in touch if you would like to learn more about my projects and interests.
